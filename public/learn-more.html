Our Methodology

01

Data Preprocessing

We process the Kepler Objects of Interest (KOI) dataset, extracting key features like transit depth, orbital period, stellar temperature, and stellar radius. Missing values are handled using advanced imputation techniques.

02

Feature Engineering

Creation of derived features such as planet-to-star radius ratio, equilibrium temperature, and transit signal-to-noise ratio to enhance the model's discriminative power.

03

Model Training

Training ensemble of 500 decision trees with bootstrap sampling and random feature selection. Cross-validation ensures robust performance across different stellar populations.

04

Validation & Testing

Rigorous testing on held-out datasets and comparison with confirmed exoplanets from follow-up observations, achieving 97.8% accuracy with minimal false positives.
